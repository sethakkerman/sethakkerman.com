Tybee Surf Poster

Poster Design, Information Graphics

Normally Tybee Island isn't known for it's great surf. This poster helped to celebrate what it had and let local surfers know the best time to go.

The concept behind this information graphics poster is that if you are a surfer, you simply want to know if it will be a good day to surf. The poster gives historical data relating to the surf reports for a certain beach in Georgia.
