SF Weekly PSA

Motion Graphics

I knew I might be on to something with the song when the recording engineer was laughing during the recording session.

This project was completed for SF Weekly. The goal of the project was to create a companion piece to go along with a cover story they published regarding stray cats in San Francisco. I chose to take a humorous approach. A draft version was featured in IdN Magazine, Volume 17, Number 5, 2010.
