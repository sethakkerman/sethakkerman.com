2012 Holiday Card

Website, Photography

Even though it was early December, the temperature still reached 71° the day of the photo shoot. The costume was not breathable.

After the positive response to our 2011 holiday card, my wife and I knew we had to take the 2012 edition to the next level. We really enjoy surprising our friends and family come December with the new concept. For the 2012 version, we decided to take advantage of my wife's outstanding sewing ability to create the reindeer costume.
