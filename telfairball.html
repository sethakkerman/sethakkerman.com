Telfair Ball

Invitation Set, Identity

Every year, Telfair Museums in Savannah, Georgia hosts its largest fundraiser/auction called the Telfair Ball. For 2010, a speakeasy theme was chosen with the title Les Années Folles, meaning “The Crazy Years.” To enhance the speakeasy theme, the save the date postcard referenced a back alley secret window with dancers seen within the mysterious space. When the patrons arrived at the museum the night of the event, they were given a card with the secret password along with their auction program. The map with the password showed where the secret even took place in relation to the place they received the password. The password is the French translation of the word charity. The only restrictions besides the title were the colors pink and gray.
