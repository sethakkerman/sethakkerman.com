Savannah Secession

Exhibition Catalog & Design

One concept we had to throw out during the planning stage was a pirate theme. “Come see Savannah’s finest booty.” As you can see from the team profile images, I brought a little bit of that into the final campaign.

This campaign consisted of the postcard advertisements and gallery catalog for the graphic design department’s 2009 senior show. The juried work consisted of both graduate and undergraduate work. I was selected to be on the team to produce these elements. In addition to designing the printed materials we were also responsible for organizing and hanging the work in the gallery.
